Our story

We didn't set out to build a pallet company.

India has two crises that don't talk to each other. A plastic waste crisis — millions of tonnes of post-consumer plastic with nowhere to go. And a logistics infrastructure crisis — where the humble pallet, the foundation of every supply chain, is still dominated by wood that warps, splinters, and dies after a dozen trips.

STRUXO was founded on a single conviction: that these two problems share a solution. We use a hot-and-cold compression process to transform recycled plastic into pallets that outlast wood by 5×, cost less to operate, and carry a verifiable environmental story.

The mission hasn't changed: build industrial products that perform better, cost less over time, and prove that sustainability is not a compromise — it's a competitive advantage.
